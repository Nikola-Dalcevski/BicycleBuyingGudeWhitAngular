Clalculate Bicycle Size

  1. Be sure of the type of bicycle you want: Mountain bike, city bike or road bike. You can read about the different bike types
  2. Take off your shoes and stand with your legs about 15-20 cm(6” – 8”) apart. Measure the height from the ground to your crotch. (B)
  3. Take off your shoes and stand with your legs about 15-20 cm(6” – 8”) apart. Measure the height form the ground to top of your head. (A)
  4. To choose a size you should stand over the frame with both feet flat on the ground. A properly sized bike frame will have 5cm(2") clearance between the top tube of the frame and your crotch. (C)
